Born in Switzerland, Lillian Elisabeth Knothe has a strong connection to Europe and the United States, where she previously lived. Lillian currently resides in Australia and has a strong artistic presence in the Pacific Rim. As an international multi-media artist and designer, her work bridges the physical and digital worlds, where her tactile and coding capacities intertwine. She has completed a Bachelor of Fine Arts (Honours) from University of New South Wales and is currently working on a Bachelor of Design Computing from University of Sydney.

Her specialties include

  • acrylic painting,
  • charcoal and pencil drawing,
  • sculpture,
  • printmaking,
  • photography,
  • animation,
  • textile design,
  • and weaving.

She has experience with

  • jewelry making,
  • woodworking,
  • fashion design,
  • tailoring,
  • ceramics,
  • performance art,
  • and oil painting.
